About Me

My name is Daniel, I am 30 years old and happily married.
I worked in chef kitchens throughout Israel for 8 and a half years. I was a sous chef several times and even managed kitchens myself.
In 2017, I began studying to be a pastry chef, including an internship in France. After that, I opened my first business.
I believe that there is no such thing as a career for life. I am always striving to learn new things in the most professional way and to expand my knowledge in as many fields as possible.
Knowledge is power! Power to broaden our horizons and show ourselves how much we are worth!
Currently, I have been working for two years at a software house for secure credit solutions as a technical support for developers.
Soon, I will be a full-stack programmer with a lot of desire to show my talent for troubleshooting and finding effective solutions in the technical field and in the digital world.
The future is going digital. Let me take part in your progress towards a more developed future!
